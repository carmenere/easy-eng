Some verbs **don't** have **continuous tenses** (`-ing` form). These include:
1. Verbs of the **senses**:
- `feel`;
- `hear`;
- `see`;
- `smell`;
- `taste`;
2. Verbs of the **perception**:
- `forget`;
- `know`;
- `notice`;
- `realise`;
- `recognise`;
- `remember`;
- `seem`;
- `think`;
- `understand`;
3. Other verbs:
- `appear`;
- `believe`;
- `belong`;
- `cost`;
- `hate`;
- `include`;
- `like`;
- `love`;
- `matter`;
- `mean`;
- `need`;
- `prefer`;
- `want`;

<br>

The verb `to have` is not used in **continuous tenses** when it means **possess**: I'`ve` got a car.
