Some verbs **don't** have **continuous tenses** (`-ing` form). These include:
- `believe`;
- `belong`;
- `forget`;
- `hate`;
- `hear`;
- `know`;
- `like`;
- `love`;
- `need`;
- `remember`;
- `see`;
- `smell`;
- `think`;
- `understand`;
- `want`;

<br>

The verb `to have` is not used in **continuous tenses** when it means possesion: I'`ve` got a car.
